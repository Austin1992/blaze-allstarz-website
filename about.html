Our Story

Built on Respect. Powered by togetherness.

PREAMBLE

"We the members of BLAZE ALLSTARZ FC a non-profit and non-political organisation do firmly and solemnly resolve to provide for ourselves a constitution and to be governed by the provisions therein contained."

Blaze AllStarz Fc is not just a football club, it’s a family founded on the principle of Respect, Fair Play, and Brotherliness. We aim to showcase elite talent while fostering a community where members live harmoniously as one family.

Aims & Objectives

1.

Provide members the opportunity to keep fit through the round leather (Football).

2.

Promote the wellbeing and interest of members.

3.

Impact positivity on members.

4.

Foster friendship and promote understanding in having respect for friendship.

5.

Provide a forum for social interaction among football lovers.
